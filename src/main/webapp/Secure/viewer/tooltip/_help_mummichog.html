• Zoom: use your mouse scroll / swipe trackpad;
  • Hover over a node to see its label and all its connections (Mouseover Effect on);
  • Double click a node to view its matched peaks and potential transformations;
  • Pathway highlight: click on a pathway name in the left table to show its compounds with potential hits. Solid circles indicate significant compounds, while empty circles indicate compounds detected but not significant. Note the KEGG global map may not contain all the compounds for certain pathways
  • View options: allow you to further customize views based on KEGG, plain, or overall expression profiles.
